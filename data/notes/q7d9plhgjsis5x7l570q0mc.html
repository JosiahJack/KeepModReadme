Blood Mace

weapon_mace

Normal Effects

Attacks with a melee swing. When off the ground, the first swipe will lunge forward and up. Lunging causes 25 damage on touch. Gibs zombies.

The attack does radius damage to all entities within a 80 radius from a point 35 units in front of the player and 18 units below the player's eyes.
