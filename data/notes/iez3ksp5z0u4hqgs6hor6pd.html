Archer Assassin

Archer knight that shoots arrows with a bow. Aims for bit before firing; will avoid firing if there is someone in the way who is friendly and wait for a clear shot.

Model is based on the archer from Hexen2. AI used is mostly Quake's with bit of Hexen2's. Fancy moves such as ducking and sidestepping are NOT used, since it is so un-Quake (and I simply want a medieval style grunt/enforcer). Holding his shot after drawing the bow, along with much of the shooting AI, was ported over. -- Patrick Martin
